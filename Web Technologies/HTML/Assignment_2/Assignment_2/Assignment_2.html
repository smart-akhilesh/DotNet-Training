Tribute to Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

A visionary scientist and 11th President of India, known as the "Missile Man".
Explore More

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. He played a key role in India's civilian space program and military missile development. He was admired for his humility, integrity, and dedication to youth education.

Major Achievements

  • Key role in India's missile development: Agni & Prithvi
  • Led India's nuclear tests (Pokhran-II)
  • Received Bharat Ratna (1997)
  • Popular author and motivational speaker
